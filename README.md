# BIOLOOM

BIOLOOM is a diverse, interdisciplinary team of researchers and students using data and ecological insight to explore how biodiversity and people are connected across the globe.

Our vision is to understand and sustain the fabric of life by illuminating the threads that weave biodiversity, human wellbeing, and planetary health together.
